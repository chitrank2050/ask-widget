# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be respectful. Be constructive. No harassment.

## Expected Behaviour

- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on the code, not the person
- Welcome contributors of all experience levels

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [chitrank2050@gmail.com](mailto:chitrank2050@gmail.com).
All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
